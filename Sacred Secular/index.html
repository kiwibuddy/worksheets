Observation
Which areas scored lowest — and why do you think that is?
Look at where your chart shape collapses inward — those are your gaps. What has made those areas feel "secular" or separate from God? Was it what you were taught? What you experienced? What you simply assumed?
Your Sacred-Secular Map
The chart updates live as you move the sliders above. A full outer shape means every area feels connected to God. Gaps show where the sacred-secular divide is strongest in your life.
Challenge
The Switchfoot singer said a pastor is not more Christian than a volleyball coach. What's your honest reaction?
Do you agree? Disagree? Feel resistance or relief? What does your gut reaction tell you about how deeply the sacred-secular divide has shaped your thinking — even without you realising it?
Your Commitment
Name one area of your life where you want God to close the gap — this week.
Romans 12 says transformation begins with renewing the mind. One specific, practical step to invite God into an area you've been keeping "secular."
